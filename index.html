Marching Spotlight!

One way to get better at Marching Band is by watching other Marching Bands that possess the "next-level" skills that we are trying to obtain! Marching Spotlight features various spectacular High School Marching Bands, College Bands, DCI groups, and possibly even WGI performances!

 

Year: 2017

Show Name: Jagged Line

Group: Bluecoats

Location: DCI World Championships

Level: DCI

Context: Bluecoats’ 2017 production, “Jagged Line,” explored the beauty of imperfection and the idea that life's most meaningful journeys rarely follow a straight path. The innovative design, paired with the corps' signature use of electronics and expansive staging, created one of the most visually distinctive productions of the season. Although the defending champions finished 5th at the 2017 DCI World Championships with a score of 95.163, Jagged Line remains celebrated for pushing the boundaries of modern drum corps design and performance.
